# pitch deck assets

## one-liner

Nook is a curated real-life social layer for people who want smaller, warmer, more intentional evenings.

## problem

People want to meet new people in real life, but most social options feel random, loud, performative, or unsafe.

## product

Nook designs small three-act social evenings with hidden facilitators, curated invites, and trust-based community growth.

## ecosystem

- Founder control room for operations and analytics
- Member app for events, bookings, referrals, and community profile
- Facilitator portal for session ops and safety notes
- Supabase backend with AI-assisted matching and insights
